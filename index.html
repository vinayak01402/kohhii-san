Our Story

Established in 2005, Koohii-san has become a beloved café chain across Tokyo, known for its expertly brewed coffee and authentic Japanese flavors. With multiple locations throughout the city, it offers a warm, inviting space where tradition meets modern craftsmanship. From rich matcha lattes to handcrafted pastries, every cup and bite reflect Japan's dedication to quality. Whether you're a local or a traveler, Koohii-san is the perfect spot to unwind and indulge.
